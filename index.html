WHAT WE STAND FOR

VentureX is a student based entrepreneurship cell that focuses on promoting entrepreneurial skills in young and creative minds.



Activities

There are a lot of technical and non technical activities that are undertaken here at Venture-X to encourage students to learn in a way which inspires them to do so.

Personality development

Soft skills are one of the most important attributes in todays world. We at Venture-X aspire to inculcate values that define us as people who are ethically adept and vision driven.

Seminars

Venture-X conducts many technical talks, business talks and seminar, so that the students get motivated and inspired




ONGOING PROJECTS



ChangePay

ChangePay originated as a NITK college startup by the founder Mr.Dyvik, which eventually started its ventures in multiple campuses across Karnataka. It is an on-campus delivery startup completely regulated and monitored by the students.

Elixir

ELIXIR is an internship program provided by SJCE STEP for the pre-final year students, which is monitored and maintained by VentureX. It is an unpaid internship for the duration of 6 months, where the students work on real-world projects based on their own innovative ideas or collaborate with on-going projects in STEP.
